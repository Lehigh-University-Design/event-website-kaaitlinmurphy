The PlayGround

Welcome to the PlayGround: Design Expo!

Our convention is a vibrant and exciting gathering of creative minds, industry professionals, and enthusiasts who share a passion for the world of toy design.

On Saturday, December 16th to Sunday, December 17th at Center120, be ready to find where design meets innovation!

WhimsiCraft

WhimsiCraft, founded by Serena Frost is known for producing high-quality, detailed, and often dystopian themed designer vinyl and action figures.

PlayHaven

PlayHaven is a toy store and gallery that features an array of niche toy designers and artists. They are known for their support of the designer toy community.

InnovaBrick

InnovaBrick is a globally renowned Italian company that has been producing interlocking plastic bricks. They are known for their innovative and creative playsets.

RainbowSpark

RainbowSpark is a prominent American toy manufacturer with have a long history of creating toys that have become classics in the industry.

Lumi

Lumi is another major American toy company known for recognized for their diverse range of toys like EcoElla, PintSizePilot, and My Little Tony.

Maya Caldwell

a seasoned toy and game industry expert who provides market research and consulting services to toy companies.

Caleb Monroe

the CEO and editor-in-chief of GPN, a leading online resource for toy reviews and information. He is a trusted source for toy reviews and industry trends.

Aria Sinclair

co-founder of PlayHaven, who has gained recognition for his unique and highly detailed action figures and art toys.

Emery Wells

a designer famous for his toy sculptures. He dissects and exposes the inner workings of popular toy characters, creating unique art pieces.

Rylan Foster

founder of the brand WhimsiCraft and specializes in creating toys that promote inclusivity, with a focus on childhood development.

Tristen Carter

runs the World Toy Experts consultancy and is a prolific writer, speaker, and blogger on toy industry trends and developments.

Lilian Holloway

ecognized for her contributions to the world of designer vinyl toys. Her work often focuses on storytelling through her creations

Max Russo

a designer and artist specializing in designer vinyl toys and collectibles. He has created numerous characters with urban art influence.

Cory Whitaker

known for his steampunk-inspired designer toys, often featuring intricate mechanical details. His work combines art, engineering, and storytelling.
